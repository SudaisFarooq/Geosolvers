üö™ What To Do

Before Flood

  • Keep food, water, and essential medicines ready.
  • Store important documents in a waterproof bag.
  • Clear nearby drainage and secure loose items.
  • Prepare an emergency contact & evacuation plan.

During Flood

  • Move to higher ground immediately; follow evacuation directions.
  • Turn off electricity and gas if safe to do so.
  • Do not walk or drive through floodwater.
  • Keep a battery radio or phone for official updates.

After Flood

  • Avoid returning home until authorities declare it safe.
  • Do not touch electrical appliances if wet or standing in water.
  • Clean and disinfect everything that got wet to prevent infections.
  • Report damages and seek assistance from local authorities.
